Some more information about our product

KVP of our product

Hello, our project is the Grocery Tracker. By putting it on one of the doors of the refrigerator you can track what grocery items you have in your fridge. When you open the door, it scans the grocery item being put in and makes a record of it on your account, which will be hosted on a website. It also keeps track of its expiration date and can notify you if it is coming up
